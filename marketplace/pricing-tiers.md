# Pricing Tiers Strategy

## Overview

Website Audit Pro offers three pricing tiers designed for different customer segments:

| Tier | Price | Target Customer | Margin |
|------|-------|-----------------|--------|
| Self-Hosted | $40 | Individual freelancers, small businesses | ~85% |
| Agency | $500 | SEO agencies, web consultants | ~90% |
| Enterprise | $2000 | Large agencies, SaaS providers | ~95% |

---

## Self-Hosted License - $40

### Target Customers
- Freelancers doing occasional audits
- Small businesses auditing their own sites
- Side project creators

### Includes
- ✅ Full source code (Next.js + Node.js)
- ✅ Deploy on any hosting (Vercel, Netlify, Railway, etc.)
- ✅ All features included
- ✅ PDF export (basic branding)
- ✅ Basic white-label (company name only)
- ✅ 6 months support
- ✅ Access to GitHub repository

### Excludes
- ❌ Remove "Powered by" attribution
- ❌ Custom domain for white-label
- ❌ API access
- ❌ Custom integrations
- ❌ SaaS mode
- ❌ Priority support (after 6 months)

### Cost Breakdown
- Platform fees (Vercel Pro): $20/month
- API costs: ~$0 (Google PageSpeed free tier)
- Support time: ~2 hours setup + occasional

**Net margin:** ~$40 - $120 = $0-80 per sale

---

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring
- Show Enterprise price ($2000) prominently
- Agency ($500) looks like a "deal"
- Self-Hosted ($40) seems like a "no-brainer"

### Bundle Strategy
- Offer Self-Hosted + 1 hour consulting: $490
- Agency + custom integration: $1500
- Enterprise + onboarding call: $1800

### Scarcity
- "Early adopter pricing" for first 30 days
- "Limited batch" for physical goods (if any)
- "Founding member" tier

## Payment Processing Options

### Gumroad
- Fee: 10% + $0.50
- Pros: Simple, built-in audience
- Cons: Higher fees, less professional

### Lemon Squeezy
- Fee: 1% + $0.50
- Pros: Lowest fees, handles taxes/VAT
- Cons: Newer, less brand recognition

### Payhip
- Fee: 5% + $0.50
- Pros: Embedded checkout, email capture
- Cons: Mid-tier fees

### Direct (Stripe)
- Fee: 2.9% + $0.30
- Pros: Full control, lowest fees for volume
- Cons: More setup, handle taxes yourself

### Recommendation
- **Primary:** Lemon Squeezy (lowest fees)
- **Secondary:** Gumroad (audience reach)
- **Enterprise:** Direct invoice (custom)
